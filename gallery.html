Favorite Travel Destinations

These photos here represent some of my favorite places I've traveled to. From a person who has lived in Iowa their whole life, I love to get away from home and travel whenever I can. Of the cities shown above, there are four within the United States (Chicago, San Diego, Tampa, Las Vegas), one in Mexico (Puerto Vallarta), and another in the Dominican Republic (Punta Cana). I would say out of all these places, my favorite city by far is San Diego, mainly because we visit family out there a lot.
